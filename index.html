Welcome To Rhema preparatory and Academy.

Shapping Tommorow's Leaders with Excellence and integrity.

At Rhema preparatory and Academy, we are committed to providing quality education that empowers our students to thrive academically, socially, and morally. Located in the Heart of Sierra Leone, Our school nurtures young minds with a focus on discipline, creativity, and innovation.

Our Services

primary School

Our Primary Section provides a strong foundation in core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Social Studies. We encourage curiosity, discipline, and independent thinking, while also developing good character and a love for learning in our Students.

Junior Secondary School

The Junior Secondary School prepare students for higher academic challenges through a balanced curriculum, covering both theoretical and practical learning. We focus on critical thinking, digital literacy, and personal growth to guide students through this important transition phase.

Senior Secondary School

Our Senior Secondary School equips students with in-depth Knowledge in specialized subject are depending on their career path whether in Science, arts, commerce or technology. We prepare students for national and international examinations, while fostering leadership, responsibility, and future-readiness.

About Rhema International School



Rhema International Prep & Academy School was Found in September 18th year 2000

Our mission is to foster a diverse and inclusive learning environment that empowers every student to achieve their fullest potential.
We are dedicated to providing a high-quality education that promotes critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.
Through collaboration with families and the community, we strive to cultivate responsible, compassionate citizens who are prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Rhema International School stands as a vibrant center for learning, growth, and possibility. Our school is built on a foundation of respect, curiosity, and academic excellence. We nurture each child's individuality, encouraging them to explore, ask questions, and push boundaries. Our dedicated teachers create inspiring lessons and a joyful, safe environment, ensuring students feel valued and empowered. Beyond academics, we cultivate character, kindness, and leadership. Rhema is a place where lifelong friendships are formed, talents are discovered, and dreams are set in motion. With modern facilities, diverse programs, and a supportive community, we prepare students not just for exams, but for life.
